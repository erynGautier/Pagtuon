Pagtuon

What does Pagtuon do?

The word pagtuon means to study in the Filipino language of Cebuano. Pagtuon grooms students from underprivileged backgrounds to become scholars by teaching them reliable study habits and giving them the chance to raise money for their college education.

How does Pagtuon work?

Choose a subject to study and earn points, coins and bucks! It's that easy. Once you earn and save enough coins, you can trade them in for cool prizes. The more coins you earn, the better the prizes will be. Plus, as you play you can earn bucks, which contribute to your college scholarship fund. What are you waiting for? Get studying!
